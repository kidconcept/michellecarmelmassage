About Michelle

Massage therapy that listens first.

Michelle Carmel Vallentyne is a certified and insured out-call massage therapist serving Monterey County.

She is trained in a variety of modalities and has worked primarily in five-star resorts. Her specialty is responding to the needs of the individual: the subtle dance between massage therapist and client.

Michelle looks forward to getting to know your body and its unique needs, then creating a session that helps you feel cared for, restored, and at home in yourself.

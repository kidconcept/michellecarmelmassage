Best for

Targeted relief with whole-body support.

The Treatment gives Michelle more room to respond to persistent tension patterns while still supporting the whole body. It is a strong fit when you want relaxation, but you also want practical relief in the places that keep asking for attention.

Inspired by therapeutic and deep-tissue service structures, this session starts with a clearer focus: neck and shoulders, back, hips, legs, feet, or the specific pattern you are carrying.

What's included

  • A brief tension-pattern check before bodywork begins
  • Focused work on one or two high-priority areas
  • Slow, deliberate pressure adjusted to your comfort level
  • Integration work so the rest of the body can settle with the focused release

Who it's for

Choose The Treatment when everyday stress has become a pattern: tight shoulders, a heavy back, tired legs, foot tension, or reduced ease of movement.
